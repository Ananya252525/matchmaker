Welcome to Coffee Roulette

Congratulations on joining our #FlavorOfCulture Coffee Roulette. This is your chance to meet a colleague from a different part of ABB and to share a bit about yourself. It’s the mix of backgrounds, together with our safe, fair and inclusive culture, that drive our shared success at ABB!

What’s Next?

  • Select your name from the dropdown list below to display the name of the ABB colleague you have been matched with.
  • Contact them directly to set up a Teams call at a time which suits you both.
  • Connect and enjoy! You might want to share some of your traditions, a song from your heritage, or some words in your language to get to know your colleague.
  • Grab a screen shot during your call and share it on Viva Engage along with what you’ve learned about another #FlavorOfCulture. Please refer to the Flavor of Culture social media toolkit if you plan to post on social media.
